Quick Reference

Flying Blue partners include Air France, KLM, Transavia (core carriers) plus all SkyTeam alliance members (Delta, Korean Air, etc.). All partners earn miles and XP, but only Air France and KLM operated flights earn UXP toward Ultimate status.

When to Fly Partners vs AF/KL

Choose Partners When:

  • Price is significantly lower than AF/KL
  • Route isn't served by AF/KL directly
  • Schedule works better for your needs
  • You don't need UXP (not pursuing Ultimate)

Choose AF/KL When:

  • You're targeting Ultimate status (need UXP)
  • Price difference is small
  • You want lounge access at AMS/CDG hubs
  • You prefer the AF/KL product
