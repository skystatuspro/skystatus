What is Flying Blue XP? Complete Guide to Experience Points

Last updated: January 2025 ¬∑ 8 min read ¬∑ Based on official Flying Blue program rules

Definition

Flying Blue XP (Experience Points) are status-qualifying points that determine your elite tier with Air France and KLM. Unlike miles (which you spend on awards), XP accumulates from flights and determines whether you reach Silver (100 XP), Gold (180 XP), Platinum (300 XP), or Ultimate (300 XP + 900 UXP) status. XP resets annually but up to 300 XP can roll over to your next qualification year.

Factor 3: Status Bonus

Your current Flying Blue status provides a percentage bonus on all XP earned:

  • Explorer (no status): No bonus (0%)
  • Silver: +50% XP bonus
  • Gold: +75% XP bonus
  • Platinum/Ultimate: +100% XP bonus

Understanding Your Qualification Year

XP operates on a personal 12-month cycle, not the calendar year:

  • Cycle start: Your qualification year begins when you first earn status (not January 1st)
  • Requalification: You must re-earn the required XP within each 12-month period to maintain status
  • Rollover: Excess XP (up to 300) rolls over to your next qualification year
  • Level-up reset: If you reach a higher status mid-cycle, your 12-month clock restarts from that date
üìä Rollover Example: You're Gold and earn 250 XP by your qualification date. You need 180 XP to maintain Gold, so 70 XP (250 - 180) rolls over. Your new year starts with 70 XP already banked.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do partner airline flights earn XP?

Yes. Flights on SkyTeam alliance partners (Delta, Korean Air, etc.) earn XP when credited to your Flying Blue account. However, only Air France and KLM operated flights earn UXP toward Ultimate status.

Can I buy XP?

No. XP cannot be purchased directly. However, the Flying Blue subscription programs (Subscribe to Miles) include bonus XP, and some credit card spend tiers earn XP.

What happens if I don't requalify?

You'll drop to the next lower status level. For example, if you're Gold and earn only 150 XP by your qualification date, you'll become Silver. There's no rollover when you drop status.
