CPM vs Value Per Mile

Don't confuse these two metrics.

  • CPM (Cost Per Mile): What you PAID to acquire the mile
  • Value Per Mile: What the mile is WORTH when you redeem it

Your goal is simple: Low CPM + High Value Per Mile. The spread between them is your "profit" or "loss" on the miles.
