How to Import Your Flying Blue Data

By Remco de Graaf ¬∑ Updated December 2025

Quick Summary

Download your PDF from Flying Blue ‚Üí Copy all text from the PDF ‚Üí Paste into SkyStatus ‚Üí Review and import. All processing happens 100% in your browser. Best results with Dutch, English, or French statements.

‚ö†Ô∏è Important: The import is a kickstart, not a guarantee

Due to variations in PDF formats, a 100% accurate import is not always possible. Always review your transactions after importing and manually adjust or delete any incorrect entries. Duplicate transactions may occasionally occur despite our detection system.

Step-by-Step Instructions

Download your PDF from Flying Blue

  1. Go to flyingblue.com and log in
  2. Navigate to My Account ‚Üí Activity Overview
  3. ‚ö†Ô∏è Critical: Click the "More" button repeatedly until ALL your activities are loaded!
  4. Click "Download" to save the PDF to your computer

Open the PDF and select all text

Open the downloaded PDF in your PDF viewer (Adobe Reader, Preview, Chrome, etc.).

Press Ctrl+A (Windows) or Cmd+A (Mac) to select all text in the document.

Important: Make sure ALL text is selected. Partial selections often fail to parse correctly.

Copy the text

Press Ctrl+C (Windows) or Cmd+C (Mac) to copy the selected text to your clipboard.

Paste in SkyStatus

  1. Go to skystatus.pro
  2. Click "Import Data"
  3. Click in the text field and press Ctrl+V (or Cmd+V) to paste
  4. Click "Analyze Data"

Review and import

SkyStatus will parse the text and show your flights, miles, XP, and other activities.

Review the data carefully, then click "Import".

After importing: Check your Transaction Ledger and manually correct any errors.

üîí Privacy First

All processing happens entirely in your browser. The text you paste is never sent to any server. Your PDF file never leaves your computer. See our privacy policy for details.

Why Copy-Paste Instead of Upload?

We use copy-paste because it gives the most reliable results. PDF files have complex internal structures that vary between:

  • Different PDF viewers (Adobe, Chrome, Preview)
  • Different operating systems (Windows, Mac, Linux)
  • Different Flying Blue PDF versions over time

By copying the text directly from your PDF viewer, we get clean, consistent data that's much easier to parse accurately.

How to change your PDF language

You can download your statement in a different language:

  1. Go to flyingblue.com
  2. Click the country/language selector (usually in the top-right corner)
  3. Select a country that uses Dutch, English, or French
  4. Download your PDF
  5. Switch back to your preferred language if needed
üí° Note: Transactions will be imported in the language of your PDF. For example, flight descriptions will show "Vol" (French) or "Vlucht" (Dutch) depending on your PDF language.

What Gets Imported

SkyStatus extracts from your statement:

  • Flight dates and routes
  • Miles earned and spent
  • XP earned per activity
  • Cabin class
  • Operating airline
  • Bonus XP (welcome bonus, promos, etc.)
  • Partner transactions (credit cards, hotels, car rentals)
  • Miles purchases and transfers

Troubleshooting

Data doesn't look right

  • Make sure you selected ALL text (Ctrl+A) before copying
  • Try a different PDF viewer (Adobe Reader often works best)
  • Check if your PDF is in Dutch, English, or French
  • Download a fresh PDF from Flying Blue and try again

Some transactions are missing

  • Did you click "More" on Flying Blue until ALL activities were loaded?
  • Very recent flights may not yet appear in your Flying Blue statement
  • Some transaction types may not parse correctly ‚Äî add them manually

Duplicate transactions appeared

  • While we try to detect duplicates, some may slip through
  • Go to your Transaction Ledger and delete any duplicate entries
  • This is most common when re-importing updated PDFs
‚ö†Ô∏è Always verify your data

The import feature is designed as a kickstart to quickly populate your account. It is not a replacement for careful data management. After every import, review your transactions in the Transaction Ledger and correct any errors.

FAQ

Is my data safe when I paste it?

Yes. All processing happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. The text you paste is never sent to any server. SkyStatus cannot see your personal information.

Why do I need to copy-paste instead of uploading the PDF?

Copy-paste gives the most reliable results. PDF files have complex internal structures that vary between viewers and systems. By copying the text directly, we get clean data that's much easier to parse accurately.

What languages work best?

The parser works best with Dutch, English, and French statements. You can change your language on Flying Blue before downloading ‚Äî use the country/language selector on flyingblue.com.

Is the import 100% accurate?

No. The import is a kickstart to quickly populate your account. Due to variations in PDF formats, 100% accuracy cannot be guaranteed. Always review your transactions after import and manually adjust any incorrect entries.

What if I get duplicate transactions?

While we try to detect duplicates, some may occasionally slip through. Go to your Transaction Ledger and manually delete any duplicate entries you find.
