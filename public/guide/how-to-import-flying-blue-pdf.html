Step-by-Step Instructions

Log in to Flying Blue

Go to flyingblue.com and log in with your Flying Blue credentials. You can also access your account via KLM or Air France.

Navigate to Activity Overview

Once logged in, go to: My Account ‚Üí Activity Overview

Or look for "My Miles" or "My XP" section in your dashboard.

Download the PDF

Look for the "More" button (three dots) or menu, then select "Download PDF" or "Activity Statement".

The PDF will download to your computer.

Open SkyStatus Pro

Go to skystatus.pro and either:

  • Click the import button, or
  • Drag and drop your PDF directly onto the page

Review Your Data

SkyStatus will parse your PDF and show all your flights, miles earned, XP earned, and bonus activities. Review the imported data to ensure everything looks correct.

What Gets Imported

SkyStatus extracts from your PDF:

  • Flight dates and routes
  • Miles earned per flight
  • XP earned per flight
  • Cabin class
  • Operating airline
  • Bonus XP (first flight, promos, etc.)
  • Miles from partners (credit cards, hotels)

Troubleshooting

PDF won't import

  • Make sure it's the official Flying Blue activity statement
  • Try downloading a fresh copy from Flying Blue
  • Check if the PDF opens correctly on your computer

Missing flights

  • Very recent flights may not yet appear in your Flying Blue activity statement.
  • For KLM and Air France flights, miles are typically added within 24 hours.
  • Partner airline flights can take longer to credit.
  • Download a new PDF after flights have posted to your account.
üí° Pro Tip: Download a new PDF periodically (monthly) to keep your data current. Each import adds new flights while preserving existing data. Use your updated data to track XP rollover and plan for status.

FAQ

Is it safe to upload my Flying Blue PDF?

Yes. Your PDF is processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. The file is never uploaded to any server. SkyStatus cannot see your flight data, miles balance, or personal information. See our privacy policy.

Why are some flights missing from my imported data?

Very recent flights may not yet appear in your Flying Blue activity statement. Miles are typically added within 24 hours for KLM and Air France flights. Partner flights can take longer. Download a fresh PDF after flights have posted to your account.

What languages does SkyStatus support?

SkyStatus automatically detects and parses Flying Blue PDFs in English, Dutch, French, German, Spanish, Italian, and Portuguese.
