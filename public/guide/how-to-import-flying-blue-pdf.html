Step-by-Step Instructions

Log in to Flying Blue

Go to flyingblue.com and log in with your Flying Blue credentials.

Navigate to Activity Overview

Once logged in, go to: My Account ‚Üí Activity Overview

Or look for "My Miles" or "My XP" section in your dashboard.

Download the PDF

Look for the "More" button (three dots) or menu, then select "Download PDF" or "Activity Statement".

  • Drag and drop your PDF directly onto the page
