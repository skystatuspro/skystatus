Flying Blue Subscribe to Miles: How It Really Works

Last updated: January 2025

Short answer:
Subscribe to Miles is a monthly Flying Blue Miles subscription. It adds Miles to your account every month. It does not provide XP, UXP, status credits or Ultimate progress.

What is Subscribe to Miles

Subscribe to Miles is a paid monthly subscription offered by Flying Blue. Each month, a fixed number of Miles is deposited into your account as long as the subscription remains active.

It is not a status product and does not influence your XP-based status, your qualification year or your Ultimate eligibility.

Pricing and cost per mile (Europe)

Subscription tier Miles per month Monthly price Cost per mile
Highest tier (Europe) 17,000 €186 €0.0109

On a yearly basis, this equals 204,000 miles for a total cost of €2,232.

What this subscription does and does not do

  • ✔ Provides a predictable monthly inflow of Miles
  • ✔ Useful for planning a known future redemption
  • ✘ Does not earn XP or UXP
  • ✘ Does not help with Silver, Gold, Platinum or Ultimate status
  • ✘ Does not guarantee award availability

Is €0.011 per mile good value

A cost of approximately €0.011 per mile is average for Flying Blue. Whether this is good or bad depends entirely on how the miles are redeemed.

Redemption type Typical value per mile Assessment
Promo Rewards (Business Class) €0.015–€0.025 Good value
Regular Business Class awards €0.012–€0.018 Marginal to good
Economy awards €0.006–€0.010 Poor
Important:
Using Subscribe to Miles for Economy redemptions often destroys value. The subscription only makes sense if you consistently redeem miles for high-value awards.

Bottom line

Subscribe to Miles is not a hack, not a shortcut and not a status tool. It is a budgeting mechanism.

At €0.011 per mile, it only makes sense for travellers who understand Flying Blue award pricing and redeem primarily for high-value awards.
