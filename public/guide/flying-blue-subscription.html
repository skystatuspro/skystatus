Subscribe to Miles pricing (Europe)

Subscribe to Miles is a monthly subscription that credits a fixed number of Flying Blue Miles to your account every month. It does not include XP, status benefits or elite perks.

Subscription tier Miles per month Monthly price Cost per mile
Highest tier (Europe) 17,000 €186 €0.0109

Over a full year, this subscription delivers 204,000 miles at a total cost of €2,232.

What Subscribe to Miles is and is not

  • ✔ A predictable way to acquire miles monthly
  • ✔ Useful if you need miles gradually for a known redemption
  • ✘ Not a status accelerator
  • ✘ No XP or elite credits included
  • ✘ Not automatically good value

Is €0.011 per mile good value

A cost of approximately €0.011 per mile is neither cheap nor expensive by Flying Blue standards. Whether this makes sense depends entirely on how the miles are redeemed.

Redemption type Typical value per mile Assessment
Promo Rewards (Business Class) €0.015–€0.025 Good value
Regular Business Class €0.012–€0.018 Marginal to good
Economy awards €0.006–€0.010 Poor

Bottom line

Subscribe to Miles is not a hack and not a loyalty shortcut. It is a budgeting tool.

At €0.011 per mile, it only makes sense if you redeem for high-value awards such as Business Class Promo Rewards. For Economy redemptions, this subscription usually destroys value.
