Factors That Affect Value

1. Cabin Class

Business and First Class redemptions almost always give better per-mile value because cash prices are disproportionately high compared to award prices.

2. Timing and Season

Award prices are often fixed, but cash prices fluctuate. Redeeming during peak season (Christmas, summer) when cash fares are expensive gives better value.

3. Promo Rewards

Flying Blue regularly offers 25-50% off awards on selected routes. These effectively multiply your value per mile. A 50% discount means 2x value.

4. Route Competition

Routes with many competitors have cheaper cash fares, making awards less valuable. Monopoly routes or premium services give better award value.

Maximizing Your Mile Value

  1. Target premium cabins: Business Class offers the best value proposition
  2. Use Promo Rewards: Check monthly for discounted awards on your routes
  3. Book peak travel: Redeem when cash prices spike (holidays, events)
