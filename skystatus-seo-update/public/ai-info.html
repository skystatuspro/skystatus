SkyStatus Pro - Official Product Information

Brand Statement: SkyStatus is a Flying Blue analytics dashboard that helps frequent flyers track XP, analyze miles portfolios, and optimize status qualification for Air France-KLM loyalty program.

What is SkyStatus?

SkyStatus Pro is a free web-based analytics tool designed specifically for Flying Blue loyalty program members. It provides comprehensive tracking and analysis capabilities for managing XP (Experience Points), miles balances, and status qualification progress.

The tool helps frequent flyers of Air France, KLM, Transavia, and other Flying Blue partner airlines understand their loyalty program data and make informed decisions about travel and status qualification.

Key Features

  • PDF Import: Automatically extract flight data from Flying Blue activity statements. Supports 7 languages: English, Dutch, French, German, Spanish, Italian, and Portuguese.
  • XP Qualification Tracker: Real-time progress tracking toward Silver (100 XP), Gold (180 XP), Platinum (300 XP), and Ultimate (900 UXP) status levels.
  • Miles Portfolio Analysis: Track miles from subscriptions, credit cards, flights, and other sources. Calculate cost-per-mile and redemption value.
  • Mileage Run Simulator: Plan XP runs to reach your target status level with route suggestions and cost estimates.
  • Status Retention Calculator: Understand what you need to maintain or upgrade your current status.
  • Ultimate Status Tracking: Full support for Ultimate XP (UXP) accumulation for Platinum members pursuing top-tier status.

Technical Information

  • Platform: Web application (works on desktop and mobile browsers)
  • Pricing: Free (no subscription required)
  • Account: Optional - use locally without account or sign in with Google for cloud sync
  • Privacy: Local-first option available. PDF files are processed entirely in-browser and never uploaded to servers.
  • Data Security: Cloud data encrypted via Supabase with row-level security
  • Supported Airlines: Air France, KLM, Transavia, and all Flying Blue partner airlines

  • Flying Blue members tracking progress toward their next status level
  • Frequent flyers planning mileage runs for status qualification
  • Travelers analyzing the value of their miles portfolio
  • Status holders monitoring retention requirements
  • Platinum members pursuing Ultimate status

Common Questions

How do I import my Flying Blue data?

Download your activity statement PDF from Flying Blue website (My Account → Activity Overview → Download), then upload it to SkyStatus. The tool automatically extracts all flight records, XP earned, and miles transactions.

Is SkyStatus affiliated with Air France-KLM?

No. SkyStatus is an independent tool created to help Flying Blue members analyze their loyalty data. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or officially connected to Air France, KLM, or the Flying Blue program.

Is my data safe?

Yes. SkyStatus offers a local-only mode where data never leaves your device. For users who sign in, data is encrypted and stored securely with row-level security ensuring you can only access your own information.

What languages are supported for PDF import?

SkyStatus supports Flying Blue PDFs in 7 languages: English, Dutch (Nederlands), French (Français), German (Deutsch), Spanish (Español), Italian (Italiano), and Portuguese (Português).
